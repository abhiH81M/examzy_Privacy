This policy explains what data Examzy collects, why, and how you stay in control. Short version: your exam data never leaves your device.

📅 Effective Nov 25, 2025 🔄 Updated Dec 29, 2025 📱 v3.3.0 🇪🇺 GDPR Compliant 🇺🇸 CCPA Compliant
01 · Local Data

Data stored only on your device

The following is stored locally and is never uploaded to our servers:

—Exam questions and answers you create
—Student results and scores
—Class and attendance records
—App preferences and settings
Your exam data stays private. We have zero access to your exams or student information.
02 · Advertising

Advertising data (free version only)

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob and partners may collect:

—Device advertising ID (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
—IP address and approximate location (country/region level)
—Device model and OS version
—App usage signals for ad relevance

Managing ad preferences: EU/EEA/UK/California users see a consent dialog on first launch. You can also go to Settings → Privacy → Advertising on your device, or purchase Premium to remove all ads and stop ad-related data collection entirely.

Premium users: No advertisements. No ad-related data collection. Ever.
03 · Analytics

Analytics (opt-in only)

If you enable analytics, Firebase Analytics collects screen views, feature usage, performance metrics, and crash reports. No personally identifiable information is ever collected.

Off by default. Analytics is never enabled for student accounts. You can toggle it anytime in Settings.
04 · GDPR

EU / EEA / UK user rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation you have the right to:

Access your data
Correct inaccurate data
Delete your data
Restrict processing
Data portability
Object to processing
Withdraw consent

Contact us below to exercise any of these rights.

05 · CCPA

California resident rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act you have the right to know what is collected, know if it is sold and to whom, opt out of any sale, and access your data — without losing access to equal service or pricing.

We do not sell your personal information.

08 · Children

Children's privacy

Examzy may be used by students of all ages under teacher supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Student accounts have analytics permanently disabled, exam data is stored locally, and no student PII is ever transmitted.
