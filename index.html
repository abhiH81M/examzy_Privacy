Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Examzy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Principle: We prioritize privacy. Examzy is an offline-first application. We do not operate our own servers to store your exam data, student results, or personal profiles. All sensitive data remains locally on your device or is transferred directly between devices using peer-to-peer technologies.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Exam Data: Questions, options, and answers you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Student Profiles: Names and roll numbers are stored locally for exam results.
  • AI Prompts: When you use the "Generate with AI" feature, the topics or keywords you enter are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate questions.

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous device identifiers (like Advertising ID) for app functionality and analytics.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous data about how you interact with the app.
  • Crash Reports: We collect anonymous crash logs to identify and fix bugs.

3. How We Use and Share Information

A. Peer-to-Peer Sharing (Local Network)

This app uses Nearby Connections API (Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and Location) to share exams and results directly between Teacher and Student devices.

  • No Internet Required: This transfer happens locally.
  • Data Encryption: Exam data is encrypted during transfer.
  • Location Data: Required by Android for Bluetooth scanning, but we do not track, store, or share your GPS location.

B. AI Question Generation

If you use the AI feature, your prompts are processed by Google's Gemini API. We do not store this data on servers.

C. Advertising & Payments

  • AdMob: Displays ads using anonymous identifiers.
  • In-App Purchases: Payment processing is handled by the App Store/Play Store. We do not access financial data.

4. Permissions Required

  • Camera: For QR code scanning.
  • Location/Bluetooth/Wi-Fi: For offline exam sharing.
  • Storage: For saving exams locally.
