Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

Welcome to Examzy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Overview

Examzy is a fully offline exam and quiz management application designed for teachers and students. We prioritize privacy and minimize data collection.

Key Privacy Points:

  • Local Storage: All exam data, attendance records, and student information are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload or sync your data to any servers
  • Third-Party Ads: Free version includes ads that may collect data for personalization
  • Privacy Control: Purchase premium to remove all ads and ad tracking

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Examzy, you may provide:

  • User Type: Teacher or Student designation
  • Name: For identification within the app (optional, stored locally)
  • Exam Content: Questions, answers, exam titles, and quiz data (stored locally)
  • Student Data: Names, roll numbers, attendance, and exam results (stored locally by teachers)

Important: All this information is stored only on your device. We do not have access to it.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

a) Third-Party Advertising Data (Free Version Only)

If you use the free version of Examzy, we display advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. Our advertising partner may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data (time spent, interactions)
  • Location data (approximate, based on IP)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views)

Purpose: To display personalized advertisements and measure ad performance.

Third-Party Policy: Google's data collection is governed by their Privacy Policy.

b) Analytics Data (Optional)

With your consent, we may collect anonymous analytics data including:

  • App crashes and errors
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Session duration

Purpose: To improve app stability and user experience.

Control: You can opt-out of analytics in the app settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Local Data (Exams, Attendance, Student Records)

  • Exam Management: Create, store, and share exams locally via QR codes or nearby connections
  • Attendance Tracking: Record and export attendance reports
  • Results Processing: Calculate scores and generate result reports

We never access, process, or transmit this data to any server.

3.2 Advertising Data

  • Display relevant advertisements (free version only)
  • Improve ad targeting and relevance
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

3.3 Analytics Data

  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand feature usage
  • Improve app performance

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We work with the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads: Displays advertisements in the free version (Privacy Policy)
  • Analytics Services: If you opt-in, anonymized usage data for app improvement

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or abuse

5. Your Data Rights and Choices

5.1 Remove Ads and Ad Tracking

Purchase Examzy Premium to completely remove advertisements and all associated ad tracking. Premium users do not have any data collected by advertising networks.

5.2 Opt-Out of Analytics

Disable analytics collection in the app settings at any time.

5.3 Advertising Choices

You can limit ad tracking on your device:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

5.4 Delete Your Data

All exam data, attendance, and student information is stored locally on your device. To delete:

  • Use the in-app "Delete All Data" option in settings, or
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Note: This action is irreversible as we do not maintain backups.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Local Storage: All exam and student data stays on your device
  • No Cloud Transmission: Data is never uploaded to external servers
  • Secure Connections: Nearby connections use encryption for local data sharing

Your Responsibility: Protect your device with a passcode/biometric lock to prevent unauthorized access to app data.

7. Children's Privacy

Examzy is designed for educational use by students of all ages, including children under 13/16.

Important for Parents and Teachers:

  • No Data Collection from Children: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • Local Data Only: Student names and results entered by teachers are stored locally, never transmitted
  • Ad-Free Recommendation: We recommend schools and parents use the Premium version to eliminate all advertising and tracking

8. International Users

Your data is processed on your device only. If you use advertising features, data may be processed by Google in accordance with their international data transfer policies.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
