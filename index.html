Privacy Policy

Examzy - Secure Offline Exams
Effective Date: November 25, 2025
Last Updated: November 25, 2025
App Version: 1.0.0

1. Introduction

Examzy ("App") is an offline exam distribution application that enables teachers to securely share encrypted exam packages with nearby students using Bluetooth/Local Network and QR code technology. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Key Points:
  • ✓ Exam data stays on your device (not uploaded to servers)
  • ✓ Ads are optional (purchase "Remove Ads" to disable)
  • ✓ Analytics are opt-in (disabled by default)
  • ✓ You can delete all data anytime via Settings
  • ✓ We comply with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and FERPA

2. Data We DO NOT Collect

Examzy does NOT collect, store, or transmit the following by default:

  • Personal identification numbers (ID, SSN, passport)
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Precise GPS location data
  • Student names (unless optionally added by teacher)

3. Data Stored Locally

All exam content and student responses are stored exclusively on individual devices:

  • Teacher devices: Exam drafts and collected student results
  • Student devices: Exam questions and submitted responses
  • No upload: No exam data is uploaded to remote servers

4. Encryption

All exam data transmitted between devices is encrypted using:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Teacher-specified 4-digit pass-code key derivation
  • PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 (100,000 iterations)
  • Random salts for each exam session

QR codes display only encrypted ciphertext, not exam content.

5. Data Retention

  • Student results auto-delete after 30 days from collection
  • Teachers can export results before auto-deletion
  • Users can manually delete all stored data at any time via app settings

6. Permissions Usage

The following permissions are requested only when explicitly needed:

  • Camera: Used only when student taps "Scan QR Code" - images never uploaded
  • Bluetooth/Local Network: Used only when teacher taps "Share Exam"
  • Location: Required for Bluetooth discovery; not used for tracking or storage
  • Notifications: Used for exam sharing status updates (optional)

7. Analytics & Crash Reporting

  • Firebase Analytics is disabled by default
  • Users must opt-in to enable analytics via consent dialog
  • No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics
  • Analytics data includes: app usage patterns, feature interactions, crash reports
  • Crash reports are scrubbed of user data before transmission

8. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Examzy displays advertisements through Google AdMob to support free access to the app.

Data Collected by Google AdMob:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size, manufacturer)
  • IP address (used to determine approximate location)
  • App usage data (session duration, interactions, crashes)
  • Ad interaction data (ad views, clicks, impressions)

Personalized Ads:

  • On first launch, you will be asked for consent to show personalized ads
  • Personalized ads use your Advertising ID to show relevant advertisements
  • You can opt-out at any time through:
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Examzy (toggle off)
    • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • In-App: Purchase "Remove Ads" to disable all advertisements
  • If you decline consent, you will see non-personalized (contextual) ads instead

  • Google Ads Privacy
  • AdMob Partners

9. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR (European Union):

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification (correction of inaccurate data)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

CCPA (California):

If you are a California resident, you have:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or shared
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale/sharing of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

To Exercise Your Rights:

  • Data deletion: Use "Delete All Data" in Settings > Privacy
  • Ad opt-out: Adjust device settings or purchase "Remove Ads"
  • Access/correction requests: Contact us at the email below

10. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Examzy is intended for educational use in school settings under teacher supervision.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent
  • Schools using this app must obtain appropriate parental consent per COPPA and FERPA requirements
  • Teachers are responsible for ensuring compliance with local education privacy laws

Parental Rights:

Parents/guardians can:

  • Review data stored on their child's device at any time
  • Request deletion of data via "Delete All Data" in Settings
  • Refuse further data collection by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us to request information about data practices

12. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Exam content encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Device-to-device communication uses encrypted channels
  • No debug endpoints or hardcoded keys in release builds
  • Regular static analysis for security vulnerabilities
  • Secure storage for encryption keys using platform keychains

14. Your Choices

  • Delete all data: Settings > Privacy > Delete All Data
