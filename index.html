Privacy Policy for Examzy

Effective Date: December 03, 2025

Examzy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Examzy.

This Privacy Policy applies to our website, and its associated subdomains (collectively, our "Service") alongside our application, Examzy. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Student Data: When you join an exam, you may provide a name or ID. This is stored locally on your device and the teacher's device for the duration of the exam session.
  • Exam Content: Questions and answers created by teachers are stored locally on the teacher's device.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, primarily for analytics and bug reporting.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you use the app (e.g., screens visited, features used) to improve user experience.

C. Permissions Requested

To provide our core features, Examzy requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used solely for scanning QR codes to join exams. No images or videos are recorded or sent to our servers.
  • Location (Fine/Coarse) & Bluetooth: Required for "Nearby Connections" to discover and connect teachers with students offline. Location data is not tracked, stored, or transmitted to us.
  • Storage: Used to save exam results, generated PDFs, and CSV exports to your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate offline exam sessions between teachers and students.
  • Generate performance reports and analytics for teachers.
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob) to support the free version of the app.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our Service.

3. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals due to the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our Service;
  • To provide the Service on our behalf;
  • To perform Service-related services; or
  • To assist us in analyzing how our Service is used.

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and unique device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your visits to this and other apps.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local Storage: All exam data, student lists, and results are stored locally on your device using SQLite and Shared Preferences.
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Clear Data" option in the app settings.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not automatically upload your exam data to any cloud server. You are responsible for backing up your own data (e.g., via CSV/PDF exports).

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & FERPA)

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

  • Teachers' Responsibility: Teachers using Examzy are responsible for obtaining necessary parental consents before collecting student data, in compliance with FERPA and COPPA.
  • Data Minimization: We encourage using pseudonyms or IDs instead of full names for students.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
